Our team won A.Team's first Generative AI Hackathon with JAY, an AI claims assistant for auto insurance.

We built it over a weekend, and it was a good example of how quickly a small team could turn LLMs into something concrete once the interfaces were finally good enough.

If you want the outside view, A.Team shared the announcement on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/ateams_were-thrilled-to-congratulate-andrew-denta-activity-7062834612862869504-Uu1C/) and published a longer recap on [how the prototype came together](https://www.a.team/insights/ai-prototype-hackathon).
